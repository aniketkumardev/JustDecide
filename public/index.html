Get Started

Planning a Decision

Establish objectives, gather relevant information, and set timelines. This step involves preparing mentally and structurally for making a thoughtful and informed decision.

Identifying Problems

Recognize and clearly define the problem. Ensure the real issue is being addressed, not just the symptoms. This clarity is critical for effective decision-making.

Problem Assessment

Analyze the problem from different perspectives. Consider causes, stakeholders involved, available data, and potential consequences of inaction.

Decision Making

Generate possible solutions, evaluate them, and choose the best course of action. Implement the decision and monitor its outcomes to ensure success.
